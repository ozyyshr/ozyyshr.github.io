 Hi!

I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), under the supervision of Prof. Jiawei Han.
I received my Bachelor's degree from the IEEE honored class, Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in 2022. In my undergraduate years, I was advised by Prof. Hai Zhao and collaborated with Zhuosheng Zhang at SJTU.
  Feel free to contact me if you want to discuss or collaborate!

 Research Interests

My research interests lie at the intersection of natural language processing and machine learning.
Generally, I’m interested in the following:
Post-training of LLMs in a data-centric sense;
Enhancing complex reasoning ability of LLMs and LLM agents;
AI for Science specifically in the domain of chemistry;
Recent News 01/2025: Two papers on AI software engineering and chemistry reasoning are accepted to ICLR 2025. 12/2024: Our tutorial on “Retrieval and Structuring Augmented Generation with Large Language Models for Web Applications” is accepted by WWW 2025. 09/2024: One paper on speculative decoding is accepted to EMNLP 2024 Findings. See you in Miami! 05/2024: OnEFET is accept to KDD 2024.
