 Hi!

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), under the supervision of Prof. Jiawei Han.
I received my Bachelor's degree from the IEEE honored class, Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in 2022. In my undergraduate years, I was advised by Prof. Hai Zhao and collaborated with Zhuosheng Zhang at SJTU.
  I am actively looking for research internships for Summer 2026. Feel free to reach out if you think I am a good fit!

 Research Interests

My research interests lie at the intersection of natural language processing and machine learning.
Generally, I’m interested in the following:
Enhancing complex reasoning ability of LLMs and self-evolving LLM agents;
Post-training of LLMs in a data-centric sense;
AI for Science specifically in the domain of chemistry;
Recent News 01/2026: Two papers on agent memory and VLM steering are accepted to ICLR 2026. 09/2025: One paper on LLM reasoning is accepted to NeurIPS 2025, and another paper on functional group reasoning is accepted to NeurIPS DB track. See you in San Diego! 01/2025: Two papers on AI software engineering and chemistry reasoning are accepted to ICLR 2025.
